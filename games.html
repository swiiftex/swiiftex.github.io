Kick, punch and dribble your way to success in LUNARBALL, your next favourite sport! Featuring 3v3 team-based gameplay, play with random queued players in Quickplay, or organise a custom match in the Server Browser.


Out now on Steam!


 
 
 


LUNARBALL uses Steam to host a P2P matchmaking system, a server browser for custom matches and an upcoming ranked MMR system! It also implements Steam's inventory service for in-game cosmetics.

 


VENATOR was heavily playtested and, although I decided not to take the project further, allowed me to learn alot about multiplayer networking and combat design. I am still keen to develop for the slasher genre some time in the future.
